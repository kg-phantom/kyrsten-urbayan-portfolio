About Me

I am a mathematics student pursuing a career in web and software development.

From a young age, I have always had a passion for art and mathematics but struggled to find an interlap between them. I have walked down the roads of math and the arts separately, exploring different facets of each. I pursued a career in the arts by majoring in Illustration. However, I missed working with numbers, functions, and formulas. Switching over to a major in mathematics, I still craved the creativity of art and design. After being introduced to coding, I was met with a solution. Why settle for just math or art when I can do both? I look forward to combining my passions for art, math, and tech in order to produce effective, creative, and entertaining projects!
